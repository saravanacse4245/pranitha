"Stay smart. Stay Safe. Stay Cyber Aware"

Cybersecurity awareness is essential in today's digital world.It involves understanding the risks of online threats like phishing, malware and data breaches and taking steps to protect personal and organizational information. Practicing strong password habits , updating software regularly and being cautious with emails and linkscan help prevent cyberattacks and keep data secure.

Tips to stay safe online

1.Use strong passwords.
2.Don't share Personal information.
3.Beware of fake links/emails.
4.Talk to your Parents if something feels wrong.
5.Don't click on unknown downloads.
6.Safe use of Social Media & Games.
7.Set Privacy settings.
8.Block/report Strangers.
9.Think before you post.
